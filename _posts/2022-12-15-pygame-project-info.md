> The game is designed to create basic financial awareness and skills amongst the youth that is targeted by this game. Therefore, the game will be set up in a way such that there will be a `cash builder` round, which will not give hazards but will have a lower level of return on rewards - this will be represented as a period of 3 months as the MAS suggests the three month savings of income. Then, a period of time shown as 3 months will be presented with a regular level of return on rewards and hazards present.  
> On screen text will only need to exist for the instructions and information welcome pages. The statistics are all numerals or in a bar display which are both universally understood between different languages.
