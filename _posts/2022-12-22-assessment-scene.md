SCENE is the acronym that we use to analyse scenes.

| Letter | Word               | Meaning                                                                                                                                  |
| ------ | ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| S      | Safety             | Assesses your scene, is their any risk to yourself, your patients, or colleagues?                                                        |
| C      | Cause              | What is the nature of injury (NOI) or mechanism of injury (MOI)?                                                                         |
| E      | Environment        | What is the environment that surrounds your patient? E.G. Lighting, Confined Spaces, Weather.                                            |
| N      | Number of patients | What is the number of patients. Are you able to handle with them yourself, or will you consider it a mas-casualty?                       |
| E      | Extra resources    | Based on your assessment do you need additional resources. Including HART, HEMS, Police, Fire, other crews, or more advanced clinicians. |
