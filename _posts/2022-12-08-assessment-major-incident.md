## Communication

Communication is vital during major incidents. There should be a Incident Commander that would have established by LAS which will communicate between LFB and MPS. It is therefore important to listen and follow instructions from the Incident Commander at all times.

## Tags

Major incidents will use tags in order to prioritise the care given to patients. This is because there are possibly more patients than the amount of personnel available.

Please work your way down in order to associate the correct tag.

| Tag Type | Condition                           | Details                                                                                    |
| -------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Red      | Massive blood loss                  | Major injuries, need immediate medical intervention                                        |
| Green    | No injuries or patient able to walk | Minor injuries, can wait for medical intervention                                          |
| Black    | Closed airway or not breathing      | Dead, or death expected soon - remove patient to designated triage area (not an ambulance) |
| Red      | Patient unconscious                 | Major injuries, need immediate medical intervention                                        |
