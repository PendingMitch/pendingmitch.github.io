DR ABC is the acronym that we use to complete a first assessment of patients. For trauma see [Trauma Assessment - DR CABCDE](/posts/assessment-drcabcde)

| Letter | Word        | Meaning                                                                       |
| ------ | ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| D      | Danger      | Assess the scene in general. See [SCENE Assessment](/posts/assessment-scene/) |
| R      | Response    | Assess for responsiveness of the patient. Use the AVCPU scale.                |
| A      | Airway      | Is their airway clear? Are there any obstructions?                            |
| B      | Breathing   | Are they breathing appropriately, are their any issues?                       |
| C      | Circulation | Is their blood flow appropriate?                                              |
